-------Casey Calle // caiiese----------------------------------------------------------------------

Hola, welcome. Here you will find all the info you need to know about my creative endeavours and all the notable projects I am involved in.

Casey Calle was born on the 4th of September 2003 in Zurich. They spent their early childhood in Madrid with their older brother, spanish father and swedish mother. In 2013 the family moved to Zurich, where they grew up and went to the Kantosschule Stadelhofen. After that the dove into the world of live sound engineering, producing and sound designing. Today they are studying Sound Arts at the Hochschule der Künste in Bern.

Casey's work is influenced by sharp, fast paced and repetitive sounds, reminiscent of the metropolitan cities they spent their formative years in. They have always dabbled in accoustic sounds like guitar and vocals, but their newer projects usually gravitate more towards synthetic soundscapes.
